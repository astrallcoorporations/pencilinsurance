Built By students, For students.

Pencil Insurance was born from a simple frustration — losing a pencil mid-exam, or finding your eraser missing when it matters most. So Arsh & Daksh built something affordable, fast, and designed for real student life. We're launching at OWIS, Bangalore on July 1 — our very first school, with more to follow.
