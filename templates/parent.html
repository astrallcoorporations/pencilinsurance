How it works

Simple as 1, 2, 3.

The whole process takes minutes. Your child does all of it — no parent action needed after signup.

01
Pick a plan
Your child signs up at pencilinsurance.com, selects their plan, and activates coverage. Takes under 2 minutes.
02
Coverage is active at school
Any covered item lost or damaged during school hours is eligible for a free replacement. Up to 5 claims per item per month.
03
Child reports on WhatsApp
Same day or next school day — your child messages us with their name, plan, and what was lost. That's it.
04
Replacement delivered to OWIS
We deliver the item directly to school within 24 hours. No trips, no forms, no hassle. The claim is logged on their profile.
05
You can track it all
Your child shares a Parent View link with you. You can see their plan, every claim, and every order — in real time.

Common questions

Parents ask us everything.

Can't find your answer? Jump to Ask PI for instant answers.

Is Pencil Insurance legitimate? ▾
Yes — it's a student-run service based at OWIS, Bangalore. Transparent pricing, no hidden fees, cancel anytime for a full refund on unused cycles. Every claim is logged and trackable via the Parent View link.
What if my child loses something expensive? ▾
The Efficiency plan (₹1,000/month) covers any stationery item up to ₹900. For items above that, contact us directly on WhatsApp — we'll assess on a case-by-case basis.
How do I verify the claims are real? ▾
Every claim is logged with a timestamp on your child's profile. You can see it in real time through the Parent View link — what was claimed, when, and the current status.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. Cancel anytime, no questions asked. Any full unused monthly cycle gets a complete refund. No contracts, no lock-in periods.
What if my child forgot to report a claim? ▾
Contact us on WhatsApp as soon as possible. Claims should be reported same-day or next school day. We always try to help if it's recent and the situation is genuine.
How do I sign my child up? ▾
Your child signs up at pencilinsurance.com/profile using a Google account or as a guest. Or WhatsApp us and we'll walk you both through it.
Is my child's data safe? ▾
We only store name, school, grade, plan, and claims data. We don't share it with anyone. The Parent View link is read-only — parents can view but not change anything.
What counts as a valid claim? ▾
Items that are covered by your child's plan, lost or damaged during school hours at OWIS, and reported same-day or next school day. Cash payouts are not available — we only replace items.
