Plain English summary: Pencil Insurance replaces your school stationery when it's lost or damaged during school hours. You pay a monthly fee, file a claim via WhatsApp or email, and we sort it out. Read the details below — if anything is unclear, just ask us.

01 About Pencil Insurance

Pencil Insurance is a stationery protection service operated by Astral.inc, designed exclusively for students. We provide coverage for school stationery items that are lost, stolen, or damaged during school hours, depending on the plan selected by the subscriber.

Pencil Insurance operates on an honour-based claims system. By subscribing, you agree to use the service honestly and in good faith.

Pencil Insurance is not a regulated insurance product under the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI). It is a student-facing subscription protection service.

02 Eligibility

To use Pencil Insurance, you must:

  • Be a currently enrolled student at a recognised school
  • Be under 18 years of age, or have parental/guardian consent
  • Provide accurate information during registration
  • Use the service only for legitimate school-related stationery needs

Astral.inc reserves the right to suspend or terminate access to any subscriber found misusing the service.

03 Plans & Coverage

3.1 Available Plans

We currently offer four subscription tiers:

  • Default (₹60/month): Pencil, Sharpener, Eraser, Pen (Butterflow)
  • Deluxe (₹100/month): Everything in Default, plus Pens (Octane), Protector & Compass, Divider & Pouch, Mechanical Pencil & Scale
  • Ultra (₹200/month): Everything in Deluxe, plus Notebooks, Branded Stationery, Markers, Coloured Pencils, Refills, and 10 sheets of paper weekly
  • Efficiency (₹1,000/month): Everything in Ultra, plus 50 sheets of paper, Premium Notebooks, any stationery item up to ₹900, Priority Support, Shading Pencils & Art Supplies

3.2 Coverage Scope

Coverage applies only to items explicitly listed in your chosen plan. Items not listed are not covered unless otherwise communicated in writing by Astral.inc.

3.3 Claim Limits

Each subscriber may file up to 5 claims per item per payment cycle. This limit resets at the start of each new billing cycle. Claims in excess of this limit will not be processed.

Claim limits are per-item, not per-account. You can claim up to 5 pencils AND up to 5 erasers in the same cycle (subject to plan coverage).

04 Claims Process

4.1 How to File a Claim

Claims must be submitted via:

  • WhatsApp: +91 98765 43210
  • Email: pencil.insurance.buisness@gmail.com
  • The Shop page on our website (for direct orders using a claim)

4.2 Information Required

When filing a claim, you must provide:

  • Your full name and registered email address
  • Your current plan
  • The item(s) lost or damaged
  • A brief description of how/when the loss occurred

4.3 Response Time

We aim to respond to all claims within 24 hours on school days. Efficiency plan subscribers receive priority handling. Claims filed on weekends or public holidays may be processed the following school day.

4.4 Fulfilment

Approved claims will be fulfilled by physical delivery of the replacement item(s) to the subscriber at school, or via an agreed pickup arrangement. We do not provide cash refunds in lieu of item replacement.

05 Exclusions

The following are not covered under any Pencil Insurance plan:

  • Non-school losses: Items lost or damaged outside of school hours or off school premises
  • Intentional damage: Items deliberately broken, damaged, or discarded
  • Third-party damage: Items damaged by another person's negligence where that person can be held responsible
  • Items not in your plan: Stationery not listed in your chosen tier
  • Electronics and devices: Calculators, USB drives, and tech items unless explicitly listed in the Efficiency plan
  • Repeated suspicious claims: We reserve the right to investigate and deny claims that appear to be fraudulent or abusive
  • Items already replaced: You cannot claim the same item twice within the same claim session

6.2 Billing Cycle

Subscriptions are billed monthly. A billing cycle begins on the date of subscription activation and renews on the same date each month.

07 Cancellations & Refunds

7.1 Cancellation

Subscribers may cancel their plan at any time by contacting us via email or WhatsApp. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

7.2 Refunds

If a subscriber has not made any claims in the current billing cycle, a full refund of that cycle's payment will be issued upon cancellation request. If one or more claims have been made in the current cycle, no refund will be issued for that cycle.

Zero-claim refund guarantee: If you haven't used the service this month and want to cancel, you'll get your money back in full. No questions asked.

7.3 Plan Downgrades

Subscribers may downgrade their plan with 7 days' notice. The downgrade takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle. No partial refunds are issued for downgrades within an active cycle.

7.4 Plan Upgrades

Subscribers may upgrade their plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. The pro-rated cost difference for the remaining days in the cycle will be communicated and billed accordingly.

08 Supply Option

Instead of purchasing stationery yourself and filing claims as needed, subscribers may opt into our Supply Option. Under this arrangement:

  • You pay your monthly plan fee to Astral.inc instead of buying items from a shop
  • We supply you with all items covered in your plan at the start of the cycle
  • Standard claim limits still apply for replacement during the cycle
  • Items supplied via the Supply Option are not eligible for cash refunds

The Supply Option is subject to availability and must be arranged in advance each cycle. Contact us to enrol.
